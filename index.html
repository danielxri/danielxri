Who am I?

I am the founder and CEO of XRI Global. I have a PhD in linguistics and have published books and research articles in both theoretical and field linguistics. I moved to Russia in my early 30's, learned to speak Russian, then did research on one of the most complex languages in the world. I worked in the non-profit sector for 6 years, deploying language technology solutions in over 15 countries.

What problem am I solving?

AI doesn't know very many languages. This means that the benefits of AI will only be experienced by those who speak these few languages. For those counting (we are) this excludes about 2 billion people.

How am I solving it?

Hiring the most talented AI engineers in the world, collecting data from every language, and building new translation systems uniquely designed to work with large language models.

Yea, but do you have any traction?

yes. We have built systems for 5 languages already. We have contracts for 50 more in 2024. We have an AI agent that can learn a language by interacting with a native speaker. We have an app that is collecting large amounts of language data all over the world as we speak.
